UTTARAKHAND

Uttarakhand, formerly Uttaranchal, state of India, located in the northwestern part of the country. It is bordered to the northwest by the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, to the northeast by the Tibet Autonomous Region of China, to the southeast by Nepal, and to the south and southwest by the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. Its capital is the northwestern city of Dehra Dun.

Intresting facts about uttarakhand

  • Uttarakhand is a state surrounded by mountains and it is also known as Devbhumi.
  • Uttarakhand divided into 13 dstricts and district are group under two division.
  • Sanskrit and hindi are the offical languages of the uttarakhand state and Garhwali, Kumaoni languages are also spoken.
  • Dehradun is the largestcity and capital of uttarakhand.
  • Devbhumi uttarakhand is also mention in the vedic purans.
  • The state of uttarakhand is divided into two divisions the first -Garhwal division and second division -Kumaon division.
  • Uttarakhand is a developing state.
